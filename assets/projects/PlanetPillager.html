Planet Pillager

Story:

“Your homeworld has become overpopulated and you need more energy to sustain life on this planet. Recently, the engineers discovered a possibility to drain and store the power of other Suns. Because this act is against the Intergalactic Court of Justice, Your people are considered criminals by The Galactic Syndicate. Therefore, the reason why they are watching you closely. We know that every planet is moving/spinning around a Sun. As a top-secret engineer, your mission is to set a target (which will be a planet), build the technology on the planet’s moon and drain the Sun power, in order to survive in this Universe. Because you can not carry materials through hyperspace, you have to use resources that you find around the target-planet. You should stay undetected as much as possible otherwise the Galactic police will chase you.”
