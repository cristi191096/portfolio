A VR interactive story

Murder House

The story takes place in a house where you are a teenager woken up by a unique sound​. The unique sound raises suspicion, meaning the character (you) decides to get up and inspect the house​. This lead to the character stumbling on what looks to be a murder scene, involving blood stains, a damaged power supply and various other clues​.
