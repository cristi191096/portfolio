Educational mobile game

Lunos

Winner of FACENS game jam in Sorocaba, Brazil this project is meant to improve digital literacy among Brazilian teachers. Our team, made of 2 Brazilian students and another 2 students from Coventry University, developt a quiz game based on self-learning and self-descovering of the electric devices and applications that people use daily. My main role in the team was programmer, where my responsabilities were: creating scripts that manage the quizes, game mechanics (for example: drag and drop system), player reward and user accounts managing systems. Beside this role I also came up with design ideas and dealt with time management.
